lnav is an advanced log file viewer for the small-scale

Interesting features - filtering , histogram display  and "online" operations - it starts searching/highlighting while you typing.

Killer combo:

lnav -n -c ';select manager, count(*) from winccoa group by manager' -c ':write-csv-to -' test.winccoa.log
